📚 BUSM 180 - Networking 101

This project is an assignment for my BUSM 180 class at Brigham Young University–Hawaii.

It was assigned by Professor PJ Rogers, emphasizing face-to-face networking and building professional connections.

Through this project, I am learning how to develop real-world networking skills, connect with peers, and expand my professional network.

💡 Key Learning Outcomes:

  • 📌 Meeting and interacting with classmates face-to-face
  • 📌 Understanding the importance of networking
  • 📌 Improving interpersonal communication
  • 📌 Documenting meaningful connections
